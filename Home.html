​Pilates

Pilates is a fitness system that focuses on core strength, flexibility, and overall body awareness. Developed by Joseph Pilates in the early 20th century, it incorporates a series of low-impact exercises and stretches performed on a mat or specialized equipment. Pilates emphasizes precise movements, controlled breathing, and proper alignment to improve posture, balance, and muscle tone. It's often used for rehabilitation and can benefit individuals seeking to enhance their physical strength, flexibility, and mindfulness. Many people find Pilates to be an excellent way to develop a strong, lean body and alleviate stress.

​Cycling

Cycling, as a form of exercise, is all about pedaling on a bicycle, which can be stationary or outdoors. It's a high-impact aerobic activity that enhances cardiovascular fitness, leg strength, and endurance. Cycling provides an excellent way to explore the outdoors, commute, or engage in competitive sports like road racing or mountain biking. It is known for its calorie-burning benefits and is accessible to people of various fitness levels. It's an enjoyable way to stay active, and for many, it's a preferred mode of transportation as well as a recreational pastime.
